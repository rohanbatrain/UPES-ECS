Enterprise infrastructure

Powered by HPE networking

The backbone of UPES-ECS is enterprise networking infrastructure from HPE — engineered for high availability, low latency, and complete local network control, with no dependence on the internet or cellular networks.

HPE Juniper SRX300 / 320
1×
The guarded edge

Firewall, DHCP, and local time source in one branch gateway. It enforces the LAN-only boundary, so emergency traffic never leaves campus.

8× GbE · zones · NAT · DHCP · NTP
HPE Juniper EX2300-C-12P
2×
The voice fabric

Fanless PoE switches power and connect every phone, access point, and server. They carry the voice network, give 111 priority with quality-of-service, and can pinpoint a caller by switch port.

12× PoE+ · 124 W · 10G uplinks · Virtual Chassis
HPE Juniper Mist AP32
2×
Campus-wide reach

Wi-Fi 6 access points carry the softphones that dial 111 — reaching every corner of campus, entirely on the local network.

Wi-Fi 6 · 4×4:4 · BLE + scanning radio

HPE Juniper Networking hardware — presented at HPE's Bangalore University Networking Day.

Resilience

When networks fail, we don't.

No internet. No cellular. No cloud. UPES-ECS runs entirely on the campus network, so the one number that matters keeps working when everything else goes down.
